The Origin

Not Built From Ambition.

Gorilla Mind was built from necessity. The founder lost his business, his home, and access to his children. He fell into the kind of darkness most men never speak about and fewer survive cleanly.

Out of that collapse — through long walks alone by the sea, cold water, structured mornings, obsessive research, and eventually professional help — he began to piece together a system for staying alive and becoming whole again.

Not motivation. Not inspiration. A real framework. A daily protocol. A set of standards to live by when you have nothing else to hold onto. He rebuilt himself using that system. He still lives it every morning.

Gorilla Mind exists because he needed it — and because he knows he is not the only one who does.
